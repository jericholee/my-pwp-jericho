PWP Milestone 2

Content Strategy:

My site will be one page and will be structured in the following order:

Navbar:

The Navbar will feature links that will take the user to the desired area of the page.

About me:

This section will introduce the subject of the website and have a picture that shows him. Inside of this section it will have information on the sales experience he brings along with a personal quote from him or a catch phrase. This will contain information on why he loves his job and how that is good for you.

Picture Slideshow Section

This section of the page will have pictures from previous sales. This will be a slideshow where the customer can click the pictures from left to right and they will loop.

Inventory Link/URL

This will simply be a link that directs the user of the site to an inventory site that is not connected to this website.

Contact Me Section

This will be a contact form at the bottom of the page. The purpose for this is to be contacted for a car purchase after providing the details requested.

Footer

The footer will contain the following information about my role as a web developer. That will be followed by my name, the year, and links to my Linked In and Github accounts.

Wireframes

The website will be formatted to be viewable on both mobile devices as well as desktop versions for maximum exposure.
