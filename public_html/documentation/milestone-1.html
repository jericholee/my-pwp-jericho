PWP Milestone 1

Purpose

This website will be to show proficiency in developing a functioning sales website to increase revenue in car sales.

Audience

This site will be geared towards consumers currently in search of purchasing a vehicle for means of work or daily travels. This caters to both business or non business needs for a vehicle and pertains to an inventory of both new and used vehicles. Target audience will include gig workers, business owners, and peers alike.

Goal

The prime objective of this site is to highlight a single sales person for the purpose of direct contact when inquiring about a for sale vehicle through a contact form. Also to increase sales while promoting the sales person. The secondary purpose of this website will be a show of work to display to others with the intention of networking, adding to a portfolio and gaining employment.

Persona

Name: Tony Programmos
Age: 41

Profession: Chief Construction Officer/CEO of Superiore Construction company that specializes in residential and commercial building jobs from start to finish. Tony successfully acquired a large commercial contract and is starting his strategy to get the job completed. He is responsible for ensuring all equipment and vehicles are ready. He currently needs one more full-size diesel truck in order to transport trailers and equipment.

Technology: Microsoft user. Using the Surface Pro X while on the job or in office. At home using a single display monitor, HP desktop and uses a Samsung Galaxy Note 10 with unlimited 4G/5G data connection

Attitudes and Behaviors: Since he has a deadline to meet, he can only allocate 2-3 days in search of the vehicle he needs. He knows exactly what he is looking for and what specifications are needed for the truck. He plans on browsing web sites first before and filling out contact forms on vehicles he is interested in. The first person to contact him back is where he plans to go.

Goals:The only goal Tony has is to fill out an online form and request for more information about a vehicle he saw online. He would like to speak to someone over the phone and have an appointment already set to make sure no time is wasted. His plan is to check each of the vehicles he saw online in person to see which is best.

User Story: I am short on time and this vehicle search will end with a purchase, I know what vehicles I am interested in and the first person who gets back to me is guaranteed a sale.
