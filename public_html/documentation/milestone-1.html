Purpose

This website will be to show proficiency in developing a functioning sales website to increase revenue in car sales.

Audience

This site will be geared towards consumers currently in search of purchasing a vehicle for means of work or daily travels. This caters to both business or non business needs for a vehicle and pertains to an inventory of both new and used vehicles. Target audience will include gig workers, business owners, and peers alike.

Goal

The prime objective of this site is to highlight a single sales person for the purpose of direct contact when inquiring about a for sale vehicle through a contact form. Also to increase sales while promoting the sales person. The secondary purpose of this website will be a show of work to display to others with the intention of networking, adding to a portfolio and gaining employment.
